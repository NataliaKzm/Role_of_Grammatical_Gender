Instructions and familiarization

Please, read the text carefully and follow the instructions provided.

We have an idea for a cartoon in which some characters are animals and objects we use in our everyday life. You will see a series of images depicting our "actors". We would like to ask you some questions about them. Your opinion will help us a lot to design our characters. There are no right or wrong answers, it is just a matter of feeling.

This character can speak, and we need to give it an appropriate voice. Decide what type of voice it should speak in - a male voice, or a female voice. Indicate your choice by selecting the letter "M" (if you choose a male voice) or "F" (if you choose a female voice).

If you feel ready, please press "Continue" to go to the next familiarization page.
